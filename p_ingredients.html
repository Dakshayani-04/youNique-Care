Recommended Ingredients for Pigmentation

Brightening Agents

  • Vitamin C: A powerful antioxidant that helps reduce dark spots and boosts skin radiance.
  • Niacinamide: Inhibits melanin transfer, brightens skin, and improves overall tone.
  • Licorice Root Extract: Naturally lightens hyperpigmentation and soothes inflammation.
  • Alpha Arbutin: Reduces melanin production to fade discoloration and even out the skin tone.

Exfoliating & Cell Turnover

  • Kojic Acid: Inhibits melanin production and helps treat sunspots and melasma.
  • Glycolic Acid (AHA): Promotes gentle exfoliation and removes dead cells to reveal brighter skin.
  • Retinol: Boosts cell turnover, fades dark spots, and stimulates collagen production (use with caution).
  • Lactic Acid: A mild exfoliant that improves texture and reduces pigmentation without irritation.

Sun Protection & Support

  • Zinc Oxide: A broad-spectrum mineral sunscreen that prevents UV-induced pigmentation.
  • Ferulic Acid: Enhances the effects of Vitamin C and E while protecting skin from sun damage.
  • Tranexamic Acid: Inhibits melanin production and is effective in treating melasma and stubborn dark spots.
