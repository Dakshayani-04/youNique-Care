Recommended Ingredients for Combination Skin

Balancing Agents

  • Niacinamide: Regulates oil production in the T-zone while hydrating drier areas and improving skin texture.
  • Green Tea Extract: Provides antioxidant protection and helps control excess sebum in oily regions.
  • Willow Bark Extract: Gently exfoliates and clears pores, ideal for oily zones without overdrying.
  • Witch Hazel (Alcohol-free): Helps tone and reduce oiliness in the T-zone while being gentle on drier parts.

Hydration Support

  • Hyaluronic Acid: Draws moisture into dry areas without making oily zones greasy; use in serum form.
  • Glycerin: A lightweight humectant that hydrates evenly without clogging pores.
  • Squalane: Provides lightweight moisture suitable for all areas of combination skin.
  • Aloe Vera: Calms and hydrates both oily and dry parts, offering a soothing effect.

Pore-Care & Protection

  • Zinc PCA: Controls oil and shine while keeping the skin balanced and calm.
  • Clay (Kaolin or Bentonite): Helps detoxify and absorb oil in problem areas without irritating dry patches.
  • Vitamin C: Brightens dull areas, evens skin tone, and provides antioxidant protection for all skin zones.
