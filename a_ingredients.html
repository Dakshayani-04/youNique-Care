Recommended Ingredients for Acne-Prone Skin

Acne-Fighting Ingredients

  • Salicylic Acid (BHA): Exfoliates inside pores to clear blockages and prevent breakouts.
  • Benzoyl Peroxide: Kills acne-causing bacteria and reduces inflammation.
  • Tea Tree Oil: A natural antiseptic that helps control acne-causing bacteria (use in diluted form).
  • Retinoids (e.g., Retinol): Promote skin turnover and prevent clogged pores (start with low strength).

Barrier Repair

  • Ceramides: Help restore the skin’s natural barrier, especially after acne treatments.
  • Squalane: A lightweight, non-comedogenic hydrator that calms and softens skin.
  • Panthenol (Vitamin B5): Soothes skin and aids recovery from acne irritation.
  • Niacinamide: Reduces oil production, minimizes pores, and strengthens the skin barrier.

Gentle Hydration & Protection

  • Hyaluronic Acid: Provides hydration without clogging pores—ideal for oily, acne-prone skin.
  • Licorice Root Extract: Reduces post-acne redness and calms inflammation.
  • Zinc Oxide: Acts as a soothing physical sunscreen that won’t aggravate breakouts.
