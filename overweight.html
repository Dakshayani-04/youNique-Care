BMI Health Guide: Overweight Suggestions

Recommended Diet

High Fiber Foods

Include fruits, vegetables, oats, and legumes to keep you full and support digestion.

Lean Proteins

Grilled chicken, tofu, fish, and eggs help build muscle and burn fat.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 2.5–3 liters of water per day to flush out toxins and aid weight loss.

Suggested Exercises

Brisk Walking

Start with 30 mins of walking daily. It’s simple and effective for fat burning.

Yoga

Reduces stress, improves flexibility and aids in healthy weight loss.

Strength Training

Build lean muscle which increases metabolism and burns more calories.
