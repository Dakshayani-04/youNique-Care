Skincare Tips for Acne-Prone Skin

Acne-prone skin requires products that control oil, prevent clogged pores, and soothe inflammation without over-drying. Use non-comedogenic, oil-free, and gentle formulas. Here's a skincare routine designed to reduce breakouts and support clear skin:

  3. Serum: Use a serum with active ingredients like niacinamide, zinc, or tea tree oil to help treat acne and reduce inflammation.

  5. Sunscreen: Always wear a broad-spectrum, non-comedogenic sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher, preferably a gel or matte finish formula.
