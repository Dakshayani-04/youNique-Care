Diet Tips for Healthy Oily Skin

What you eat can greatly influence oil production and breakouts. For oily skin, focus on a diet that reduces inflammation, controls excess sebum, and keeps pores clear.

Water-Rich Foods

Cucumber, celery, and watermelon help flush out toxins and maintain skin hydration, balancing oil levels naturally.

Healthy Fats

Avocados, flaxseeds, and walnuts are rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which help reduce inflammation and regulate oil production.

Antioxidant-Rich Foods

Leafy greens, berries, and green tea contain antioxidants that fight acne-causing free radicals and support clearer skin.


Probiotic Foods

Yogurt, kimchi, and kombucha support gut health, which can help reduce acne and oily flare-ups from the inside out.
