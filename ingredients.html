Recommended Ingredients for Sensitive Skin

Soothing Agents

  • Centella Asiatica: Calms irritation, promotes healing, and strengthens the skin barrier.
  • Aloe Vera: Reduces redness and inflammation while providing light hydration.
  • Allantoin: Softens and protects the skin while soothing irritation.
  • Colloidal Oatmeal: Provides anti-inflammatory relief and hydration for itchy, sensitive skin.

Barrier Repair

  • Ceramides: Restore and maintain the skin’s natural protective barrier.
  • Squalane: Mimics skin’s natural oils to moisturize without irritation.
  • Panthenol (Pro-vitamin B5): Supports skin repair and enhances hydration.
  • Niacinamide: Reduces redness, improves texture, and strengthens the skin barrier (use in low concentration).

Gentle Hydration & Protection

  • Hyaluronic Acid: Provides hydration without clogging pores or causing sensitivity (use low molecular weight if needed).
  • Licorice Root Extract: Helps soothe inflammation and even out skin tone.
  • Zinc Oxide: A mineral sunscreen ingredient that protects sensitive skin from UV rays without irritation.
