Diet Tips for Dry Skin

Healthy Fats

Foods rich in healthy fats like avocados, olive oil, and almonds help strengthen the skin barrier and retain moisture.

Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Salmon, flaxseeds, and walnuts reduce skin dryness by supporting lipid production and reducing inflammation.

Water-Rich Foods

Incorporate cucumbers, melons, and leafy greens to stay hydrated from the inside and support skin elasticity.

Vitamin E-Rich Foods

Vitamin E from sunflower seeds, spinach, and avocados acts as an antioxidant, protecting skin cells and locking in moisture.
