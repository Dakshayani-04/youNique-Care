Recommended Ingredients for Oily Skin

Oil Control Agents

  • Salicylic Acid: A BHA that exfoliates inside pores to reduce oil buildup and prevent acne.
  • Niacinamide: Helps regulate sebum production, minimize pores, and improve skin texture.
  • Tea Tree Oil: Offers antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties to fight acne and reduce oiliness.
  • Witch Hazel: A natural astringent that helps control oil and tighten pores without over-drying.

Lightweight Hydration

  • Hyaluronic Acid: Hydrates the skin without clogging pores, especially in gel-based formulas.
  • Aloe Vera: Calms the skin while providing non-greasy moisture and reducing excess oil.
  • Green Tea Extract: Contains antioxidants that reduce inflammation and help balance sebum production.
  • Panthenol (Pro-vitamin B5): Hydrates and soothes oily skin without adding heaviness.

Acne & Shine Control

  • Zinc PCA: Reduces oiliness and helps treat acne by controlling sebum and bacteria.
  • Kaolin Clay: A gentle clay that absorbs excess oil and detoxifies pores.
  • Charcoal: Deep cleans pores by removing impurities and controlling shine.
