BMI Health Guide: Underweight Suggestions

Recommended Diet

Calorie-Dense Snacks

Include nuts, seeds, dried fruits, and granola bars for healthy weight gain.

Protein-Rich Meals

Consume lean meats, eggs, dairy, tofu, and protein shakes to build mass.

Frequent Meals

Eat 5–6 smaller meals a day to ensure a consistent calorie intake.

Suggested Exercises

Strength Training

Focus on resistance exercises to help increase muscle mass and strength.

Light Cardio

Include low-impact cardio like cycling or brisk walking to improve endurance.

Yoga

Enhances flexibility, posture, and digestion—great for overall health.
