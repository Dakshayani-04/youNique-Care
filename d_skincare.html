Common Causes of Dry Skin

  • Cold or dry weather conditions.
  • Hot showers or baths that strip natural oils.
  • Harsh soaps or cleansers.
  • Dehydration or inadequate water intake.
  • Medical conditions like eczema or psoriasis.
