Diet Tips for Healthy Acne-Prone Skin

Your skin reflects your internal health. For sensitive skin, an anti-inflammatory and antioxidant-rich diet can help reduce flare-ups and promote a healthy skin barrier.

Water-Rich Foods

Cucumber, aloe vera, and watermelon help soothe and hydrate sensitive skin from within, reducing redness and irritation.

Healthy Fats

Foods rich in omega-3 fatty acids such as avocado, flaxseeds, and walnuts help calm inflammation and strengthen sensitive skinâ€™s natural barrier.

Antioxidant-Rich Foods

Blueberries, strawberries, and spinach are rich in antioxidants, which protect sensitive skin from environmental stress and free radical damage.

Probiotic Foods

Yogurt and fermented foods like kefir and kimchi support gut health, which in turn helps reduce inflammation and flare-ups in sensitive skin.
