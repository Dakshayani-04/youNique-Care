Recommended Ingredients for Dry Skin

Deep Hydration

  • Hyaluronic Acid: Attracts moisture and helps retain hydration.
  • Glycerin: Humectant that pulls water into the skin.
  • Urea: Gently exfoliates and hydrates dry skin.
  • Squalane: Lightweight oil that moisturizes and soothes.

Barrier Protection

  • Ceramides: Rebuild the skin barrier and prevent moisture loss.
  • Shea Butter: Rich emollient for deep moisturization.
  • Petrolatum: Locks in moisture and protects against dryness.
  • Beeswax: Forms a protective barrier to seal hydration.

Soothing & Nourishing

  • Aloe Vera: Soothes irritation and lightly hydrates.
  • Colloidal Oatmeal: Calms itchy, dry skin.
  • Panthenol (Vitamin B5): Repairs and softens dry, flaky skin.
  • Avocado Oil: Rich in vitamins and fatty acids for nourishment.
